Artificial Intelligence (AI) is transforming industries by enabling machines to perform tasks that normally require human intelligence. Machine learning, a subset of AI, allows computers to learn from data and improve their performance without explicit programming. Deep learning techniques have significantly advanced image recognition, speech recognition, and natural language processing. AI applications include healthcare, finance, education, autonomous vehicles, and customer service. As AI technology continues to evolve, it is expected to improve efficiency, automate repetitive tasks, and assist humans in making better decisions across various domains.
